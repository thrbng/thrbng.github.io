Privacy Policy — PropEasy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

PropEasy ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. Our core application does not collect, store, or share your personal information. However, third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) integrated into the app may collect certain data as described below.

1. Information Collection

PropEasy does not collect or process personal data. The app works without requiring you to sign up, provide an email, or submit personal information.

2. Advertising

PropEasy displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and process data for ad delivery and personalization according to their privacy practices. For details, see Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt-out of personalized ads from Google AdMob: Adjust your Google Ads Settings or change preferences in your device settings.

3. Children

This app is not directed to children under 13. While we don't collect data, AdMob may process information from all users. If you believe a child has used this app, contact Google directly regarding AdMob data practices.

4.Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU/California), you may have rights to: access, delete, or opt-out of data sale. Contact us to exercise these rights. Note: PropEasy doesn't sell data, but AdMob data falls under Google's policy.

5. Data Retention

PropEasy retains no user data. Google AdMob retains collected data according to their policy, typically 9-18 months for advertising metrics.
